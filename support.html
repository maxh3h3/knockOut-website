❓ Frequently Asked Questions

🔄 How does spaced repetition work?
Our algorithm schedules flashcard reviews based on your performance. Cards you find difficult appear more frequently, while mastered cards are reviewed less often for optimal retention.
🏆 How do I unlock new lessons?
Complete prerequisite lessons or use gems to unlock content early. Each lesson type (Story → Grammar → Reading) follows a structured progression path.
💎 What are gems and how do I earn them?
Gems are earned by completing lessons and achieving milestones. Use them to unlock lessons early or bypass certain requirements.
📖 Can I create my own flashcards?
Yes! Create custom decks, add your own words and translations, and study them using our spaced repetition system.
📱 Is my progress saved across devices?
Your progress is synced to your account and available across all your devices when logged in.

🛠️ Troubleshooting

📶 App won't sync or load content
  1. Check your internet connection
  2. Close and restart the app
  3. Log out and log back in
  4. If problem persists, contact support
🔄 Lost progress or missing data
  1. Ensure you're logged into the correct account
  2. Check if you have multiple accounts
  3. Wait a few minutes for sync to complete
  4. Contact support with your account email
💥 App crashes or freezes
  1. Update to the latest app version
  2. Restart your device
  3. Clear app cache (if available)
  4. Report crash details to support
💾 System Requirements:
